What is the new technology FHIR ?

FHIR is a standard

HL7 FHIR is the newest international healthcare standard allowing third party apps to securely access EHR data in real time.

FHIR is a community 

FHIR is not only a standard but a large and international community sharing knowledge, tools and code.

FHIR is an ecosystem

Just as the application store on smartphones changed the way companies develop apps and integrate them into a larger environment, FHIR allows emerging app gallery like SMART on FHIR.

FHIR is taking off

The Argonaut Project is an implementation community comprising leading US technology vendors including Accenture, Apple, Cerner, Epic and provider organizations to accelerate the use of FHIR.

FHIR is open

FHIR specification is open source and available on the HL7 FHIR website.

FHIR is secure

Users and clients accessing data must be authenticated. We recommend OPenID Connect and OAuth using the Smart on FHIR profile
